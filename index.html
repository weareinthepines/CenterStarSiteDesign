Our Company

By leveraging assets and relationships, we are uniquely able to provide construction services virtually anywhere. Our ability to source resources and equipment directly reduces costs and streamlines the process, while our expert design teams are uniquely qualified to overcome any challenge to meet the client’s expectations. We provide the technical guidance and resources to control expenses. With all trades orchestrated under one management team, ensuring compliance with environmental concerns, manufacturer warranties and industry standards.

The industry’s demand for alternative energy sources has increased year after year. Environmentally intelligent and highly efficient options are of vital necessity to energy professionals, owners and operators. Long-term solutions, reliable energy supply and dependable service are essential to success.

CenterStar Energy LLC., is a master solar integrator, with the expertise, knowledge, assets and resources to manage every aspect of design, build, installation including ongoing operations and maintenance. CenterStar delivers innovative solutions, expert service and advanced systems, designed to maximize return on investment. The company has national capabilities with the ability to execute projects of any size, in any region.

CenterStar Energy was devised from the idea to deliver complete renewable energy resources to customers. One successful project at a time in order to make the world a better and more green planet. The new corporate brand was born from the model of the Sun serving as the one true energy source to sustain all life. CenterStar was formed to address increasing demand for alternative energy and to support ever evolving industry requirements. We have the technical proficiency to effectively implement all our customers’ needs, to achieve energy efficiency objectives and the resources to support operations for the life of your energy system.

Our History

CenterStar Energy is lead by solar renewable industry leaders including over 75 expert staff and field technicians exceeding 35 years industry experience. With hundreds of successful projects in place and installing well over 1.5 GW’s in solar power, our company provides a wide breadth of resources unsurpassed in the industry. We have partnered with respected solar energy and utility companies including Sun Edison, Washington Gas Energy Systems, SoCore Energy and others.

Today the company is privately held and holds all necessary licenses, insurance, bonding, credit and safety certifications required to facilitate all project requirements. CenterStar is structured as an open-shop/merit shop company. Although our primary goal is to operate and service our clients across the country including US territories as an open-shop company, in many jurisdictions we also provide prevailing wage, as well as union shop labor where needed. We are currently an Army Corp. of Engineers approved installer, included on the GSA schedule and a MATOC approved teaming contractor.
